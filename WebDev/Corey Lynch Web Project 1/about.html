About Us

Corey's Campaign is a non-profit organisation set up to help raise awareness about plastic production and plastic use in order to aid the environment. The organisation is completely profit free and any earnings/donations and funded straight back into the organisation in order to futher generate awareness. Our main goal is to spread awareness about the negative effects of everyday plastic use and how it has a detrimental effect on our environment.

Corey's Campaign was founded during the summer of 2017 and since then has organised and held over 500 events in 52 countries worldwide in order to raise awareness to save our environment and ecosystems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Corey's Compaign a profitable organisation?
No, Corey's Campaign is a non-profit organisation set up with the intention of raising awareness for the environment.
Where is Corey's Campaign based?
Corey's Campaign was originally founded and based in Ireland, however in recent years we have set up numerous workshops around the world.
How can I get in contact with Corey's Campaign?
You can get in contact with one of our volunteers by visiting our Contact Us page. One of our staff will be happy to help!
When was Corey's Campaign founded?
Corey's Campaign was founded during the summer of 2017 in Cork, Ireland by Corey Lynch. It was set up with the intention of raising awareness for the environment.
