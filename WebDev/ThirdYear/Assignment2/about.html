Frequently Asked Questions

How did PTS Motorcycles start?

We started as a small business in 2006. Our primary line of work then was breaking bikes for parts. Since then our business has grown exponentially and we have branched out into various lines of work. We now do everything from supplying bike parts worldwide to servicing and repairing machinery on-site. We even have our own Motocross team! So rest assured we are total petrol heads, just like yourself!

Do PTS Motorcycles only offer service and repairs?

Absolutely not! We have a fully stocked store on-site. We offer a range of motorcycle gear from top-tier brands. We stock both on-road and off-road gear such as boots, gloves, textiles, leathers, jerseys and helmets! If there is something in particular you are looking for, we can order with next day delivery at competitive prices that won't be beaten!

What do PTS Motorcycles specialise in?

Here, at PTS Motorcycles, we specialise in the service and repair of motorcycles, UTV's and ATV's. We are happy to assist you regardless of your machinery. We are experinced working with road motorcycles of all engine displacemnts and styles. We also specialise in MX Bikes, Quad Bikes and Off-Road Utility Vehicles.
