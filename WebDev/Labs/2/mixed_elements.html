Additional Elements Used In HTML

The following sections are examples of elements that can be added to a web page.
The Earth gets one hundred tons heavier every day due to falling space dust

Superscript and Subscript

These elements allow us to change content so that it displays above (sup) or below (sub) the normal line of text.

On the 4th of September you will learn about EMC2

The amount of CO2 in the atmopsphere grew by 2ppm in 20091

Quotations

Two elements are commonly used to marking up quotations: Blockquote and q.

Example of Blockquote

This is a standard paragraph. The next paragraph is enclosed within the Blockquote element.

Did you ever stop to think, and forget to start again?

Example of Quotes(q)

As A.A. Milne said, Some people talk to animals. Not many listen though. That's the problem.

Abbreviations and Acronyms

Both use the abbreviations element (abbr)

Prof Stephen Hawking is a theoretical physicist and cosmologist. NASA do some crazy space stuff.

Citations amd Definitions

The (cite) element can be used when you are referencing a piece of work (e.g. book, research paper, film) to indicate where it is from.The (dfn) element is used to indicate the defining instance of a new term.

A Brief history of Time by Stephen Hawking has sold over ten million copies worldwide.

A black hole is a region of space from which nothing, not even light, can escape.

Changes to Content: Inserting and Deleting Text
